What is the Inclusion Council

In response to a growing awareness of the need to support underrepresented communities in finance, ECMG launched the Inclusion Council in the summer of 2020. This council was founded in order to create a more diverse and equitable environment within the organization. One of the defining goals of ECMG is to be an equal opportunity student-run organization that delivers a high standard of financial knowledge to its members and the community it operates in.
